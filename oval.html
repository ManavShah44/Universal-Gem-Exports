What is a heart-cut diamond?
Just like it sounds, a heart-cut diamond is a diamond in the shape of a heart. In order to create the rounded head and pointed end of the perfect heart shape, cutters need a stone that is completely symmetrical.
This means it can be shaped into a pear cut, before having a cleft carefully added to create its characteristic shape. Experts recommended that any diamond selected for a heart cut is above a 0.50 carat in size so the shape can be clearly seen in 3D.
 evidently going beyond the current industry standards of 4C evaluation.
